Beasts

They're past the point of no return. Changed, but not how the elders do it, these ones went feral. Are they a cancerous growth and a threat to all they held dear, or just another step of evolution?

Last updated 28.03.21

Huskers

They will strip your shell from your carcass, make you a decoration, a colorful husk. Shore tribsmen shells are valuable for their peculiar mutations, sheer size and variaty.

Last updated 28.03.21

Searching Parties

Ishni's exiled were thrown out into the wasteland because they were an inconvenience to deal with back at home. But some ishni flew intentionally, betting on a streak of luck to survive the harsh environment, running away from their masters. Searching parties and bounty hunters from over the mountains are hunting for those that Inshi want back dead or alive. Its not a necessity, its a matter of principle.

Last updated 28.03.21
