MILITIA

Though your family is established, they remain ever-vigilant. Guard patrols will tell you if anything hostile crosses your borders.

SHOW OF FORCE

If you have both Surplus: Weaponry and Leadership, you gain +1 Reach. Your military might reinforces your diplomacy and influence.

COLD SILVER

Start with 3-Silver. When you go all-out on an enemy, you can spend 1-Silver to:

  • Dismay and overwhelm your foes.
  • Drastically alter the battlefield or your place in it.
  • Leave the battlefield in whateverdirection you wish.
At the start of a new Age, reset to 3-Silver.

SCHOOL OF THE BROKEN SWORD

It is believed that, if the sword is a precise weapon, and silver its made of is a structured and sturdy matter, the edge of a broken sword exposes the inner chaotic rugged structure of it. If you cast magic through that sword it will be broken down into chaotic sub energies raging into different directions. So if a sword is a sniper rifle, broken sword is a shotgun. Studies of that institute aim at figuring out what magic is made of and how to split it into sub energies. Like taking an energy from a pinpoint lightning blast and breaking it down to slowly power a furnace for months.

MUTUAL PROTECTION

"You mess with him you mess with all" mentality. If somebody hurts you they can expect heavy pushback. When you defend another community against predation, you gain Surplus: Justice, Spies, or Morale.

FRESH EYES

The people of this land have biases and perspectives they may not even be aware of. You’re able to see past those, and find opportunities they might have missed. And can reinterpret rituals and rites to suit their agenda, and see problems and events from a different perspective, if only someone listened.

SHUNNED LABOUR

You work jobs mainstream society is unwilling to perform. At the start of each season, this gives you Surplus: Coin. Pick what defines the work:

  • It’s strenuous grunt work. At the start of each season, gain Need: Recruits if you don’t have it already.
  • It breaks mainstream society’s taboos. At the start of eachseason, gain Need: Prestige if you don’t have it already.
  • It’s really bad for your health. At the start of each season, gain Need: Medicine if you don’t have it already.

BROTHERS TO BEASTS

Each family member is ritually bonded to a chosen familiar.

ISOLATION

Your people are dispersed, but their bonds stay strong across vast distances. Their familiars carry messages and tell signs.

STALK THE LAND

Bonded with their familiars, your rangers roam the land unseen and unchallenged. When you gain one of the specified resources, put a token on the map. At any time you can reveal the marker to perform an action appropriate to the surplus it represents:

  • Surplus: Hunters. If there’s a battle in their vicinity, you may give an involved Family or Faction Need: Recruits.
  • Surplus: Spies. If someone attempts diplomacy or espionage in their vicinity, you gain 1 Data.
  • Surplus: Scouts. If you Uncover Secrets in their vicinity, pick 2 details to add to the map. If you put them in an unexplored area, gain 1 Data.
  • Surplus: Traders. If a community overflows with resources in their vicinity, you gain the erased Surplus.
Once a marker’s revealed to the group, they’re vulnerable to attack: if they’re wiped out, you lose the Surplus. You can move them with a few day’s notice, or sooner by triggering Hold Together.

NOMADS IN TRADE

A big portion of population is constantly on the move. Desert hides precious materials for them to harvest, but gives no means to live. The caravans sustain themselves with trade, moving from lake to lake. Surveying the land on site, exploiting whatever resources they can find, and move on.

TRADE WITH FOUR CORNERS

The web of trade reaches every corner of this peninsula. When they send their traders out for a particular luxury, they roll +Reach; on a hit they get what they want, but on a 7-9 it’s not all they hoped for – or there’s strings attached.

STOCK IN TRADE

Ishni's travellers have a particular product range they trade in, and you can expect them to have those wares around unless they are deeply in need. Once per age, Ishni can draw on one of they stocks in trade to boost a roll’s results by one category: 6- to 7-9, 7-9 to 10+.

BRAND LOYALTY

When ishni would use diplomacy to meet someone who’s bought from them before, instead they automatically get an audience, which will always be willing to at least hear them out.
